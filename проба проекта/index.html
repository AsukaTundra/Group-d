Thank you for visiting us!

Tuning studio founded in a small garage by several young teenagers at the turn of 1997. Since then, we have achieved the current result with hard efforts, now the cars prepared by us successfully participate in the Blancpain GT Series Endurance Cup, NASCAR, RDS, Formula Drift, Drift Masters and other world competitions. We are proud of the way we have passed. Every day our team improves its skills, learns something new and progresses so that you can entrust us with your car. We do everything to ensure that our customers are confident both in their car and in their safety in it. We will be happy to work on your car.

Nissan Silvia S13 "GROUP-D" for Iliasville von Einzbern under the regulations of Drift Masters. Under the hood, an atmospheric 8-cylinder V-shaped engine with a volume of 7.4 liters produces 765 horsepower and 960 torque on the AEM INFINITY 506 block. The TTi gearbox is 5 with an additional 4.5 gear. The carbon gimbal transmits power to the reducer Holinger. N1 levers and PoweredByMax fists are responsible for handling. DG5 racks. The carbon-Kevlar body kit was designed by our designer taking into account the wishes of the client, the inspiration was the Audi Quattro Group-B. NASCAR safety framework and fire extinguishing system. You can consider in more detail in WORKS

Mitsubishi Lancer Evolution IX "AUGERTORQUE". Annual participant of Pikes Peak. 3-time champion of the American racing division of the prototype series. The lightweight body, carbon-Kevlar hinged elements and the 4G63T aluminum block allow the 4-door sedan to have a mass of 993 kilograms, thanks to which an incredible 600 horsepower is able to accelerate the car to the first hundred in an incredible 1.7 seconds. Samsonas gearbox, Holinger gearbox. Its own suspension is made of aluminum. The huge aerodynamic elements configured in the air tube equip the car with an additional 1300 kilograms of weight for downforce. You can consider in more detail in WORKS

Volvo 242. One of the few non-racing cars that have been in our garage. Restored to factory condition by our team. A 5 cylinder 20 valve 2.2 liter Audi 20V AAN engine is installed. The BorgWarner EFR 7670 turbine, PLAZMAMAN exhaust manifold and 3.5-inch exhaust system allow the engine to show 420 horsepower on the dinostend. A 12-gallon sports fuel system is also installed. Reinforced ZF 310 box. The suspension is standard, with reinforced springs and coilovers. The wider fenders allow the 17×9-inch Weds sport TC 105 X wheels to be positioned at all four corners. Excellent appearance, and no less excellent filling. You can consider in more detail in WORKS
